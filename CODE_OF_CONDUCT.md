# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We as members, contributors, and leaders pledge to make participation in our project and community harassment-free.

## Our Standards
Positive behaviors:
- Respectful communication
- Inclusiveness
- Accepting constructive feedback

Unacceptable behaviors:
- Harassment
- Trolling
- Publishing private information

## Enforcement
Report issues by opening an Issue in this repository.
